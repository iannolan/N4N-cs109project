Introduction

What makes a politician appealing?

In asking this question, our team wasn't so much focused on looks, dress, even the timber of the voice, but rather the manner in which a politician is able to convey their ideas and goals to the public in a meaningful way.

Of course, when it comes to talking about messages and the ways in which they're transmitted, the medium of text is the first to come to mind, despite the obvious importance of additional linguistic cues like body langauge or intonation. In the end, it's the words one uses that determines how their ideas are received.

So, in order to work towards creating a tool or resource that might benefit politicians in terms of figuring out what sorts of ideas to stress in their appeals to the public as well as how to express these ideas, our team decided to turn to Reddit and figure out what favorably rated political comments could tell us about these factors. Those of you with a bit less time on your hands might want to check out our (admittedly somewhat silly) summary video below for the gist of things.
